🗺️ Ancient Bihar — Kingdoms & Mahajanapadas

Aryans came to Bihar in the LATER Vedic period using IRON tools. NOT early Vedic period! Also: Vajji republic had 8 clans — NOT 4, NOT 6.

Four Major States of Bihar (6th Century BC) 🔥

Videha (Mithila)
North of Ganga. Dynasty from Ikshvaku's son Nimi Videha (founder). Famous king Janaka (father of Sita). Became insignificant before rise of Buddhism.
Anga
First mentioned in Atharvaveda. Modern Bhagalpur + Munger. Capital: Champa (also called Malini). Hieun-Tsang = Chenpo. Conquered by Bimbisara.
Vajji (Republic)
Union of 8 ruling clans. Lichchavis most important. Capital: Vaishali. Governed as Sangha/Gana. World's first republic-type institution.
Magadha
Patna + Gaya districts. First capital: Girivraja/Rajagriha. Became greatest empire. First mentioned in Atharvaveda.
Four states: VAVM = Videha, Anga, Vajji, Magadha. "Very Ancient Valiant Men of Bihar!"

👑 Magadha Dynasties — All Rulers

Bimbisara = REAL founder of Magadha's power (Haryanka). Brihadratha founded the first ever dynasty. Kalasoka (not Udayin) PERMANENTLY moved capital to Pataliputra. Udayin only first shifted there!
RulerDynasty / PeriodKey Facts
BrihadrathaBrihadratha dynastyEldest son of Chediraja Vasu. Founded FIRST Magadha dynasty. Capital: Girivraja/Rajagriha. 22 kings after Sahadeva. Last = Ripunjaya (killed by minister Sunaka).
Bimbisara 🔥Haryanka (544–492 BC)REAL founder of Magadha's greatness. Conquered Anga (defeated Brahmadatta). Married Kosala + Vaishali (Chellana) + Madra princesses. Gifted Venuvana to Buddha. Sent physician Jivaka to Avanti king. Contemporary of Buddha AND Mahavira.
Ajatashatru 🔥Haryanka (492–460 BC)Childhood name: Kunika. Killed father Bimbisara. Invented 2 new weapons: Rathamusala + Mahasilakantaka. Sent Vassakara to sow dissension among Lichchavis. Built fort at Patali village. Buddha + Mahavira BOTH died in his reign. Ruled 32 years.
Udayin (460–444 BC)HaryankaTransferred capital from Rajagriha to Pataliputra (Kusumpura) in 4th year. Confluence of Ganga & Son. 🔥
NagadashkaHaryanka (LAST)Last Haryanka ruler. Overthrown by Shisunaga dynasty.
Shisunaga (412–394 BC)ShisunagaFounded Shisunaga dynasty. Capital: Vaishali. Destroyed Pradyotas of Avanti (annexed Avanti to Magadha). 🔥
Kalasoka (394–366 BC) 🔥ShisunagaAlso Kakavarna (Puranas). PERMANENTLY moved capital to Pataliputra. 2nd Buddhist Council at Vaishali in his reign.
Mahapadma Nanda (344–334 BC) 🔥Nanda (founder)Founder of Nanda dynasty. Claimed ekarat — sole sovereign. First to conquer Kalinga. Brought Jina image from Kalinga as trophy.
Dhana NandaNanda (LAST)Last Nanda ruler. Contemporary of Alexander. Greek writers = Aggrammes/Xandrames. Overthrown by Chandragupta Maurya.

Why Magadha Succeeded

  • First capital Rajgir surrounded by five hills — naturally fortified
  • Rich iron deposits in nearby forests — superior weapons
  • Fertile land in Gangetic plains — strong agricultural base
  • Pataliputra at Ganga-Son confluence — controls trade routes
  • Use of elephants in warfare — decisive military advantage
  • Succession of able kings — Bimbisara, Ajatashatru, Chandragupta built systematically
Magadha's success = FIRES: Fortified hills + Iron + Rich soil + Elephant power + Strategic location + Rulers!

Ashoka (273–232 BC) — Key Facts 🔥

  • Kalinga War (261 BC) — only major war. 100,000 killed, 150,000 deported.
  • Converted to Buddhism by Nigrodha — a 7-year-old boy monk (per Mahavamsa).
  • Sent son Mahendra + daughter Sanghamitra to Ceylon as missionaries.
  • 3rd Buddhist Council at Pataliputra under Moggaliputta Tissa.
  • Edicts: Brahmi script + Prakrit language (Kharosthi in NW).
  • Personal name in edict = Maski edict. Name "Dharmashoka" = Sarnath inscription. 🔥
  • Reverence for Buddhism = Bhabru edict. Claims emperor of Magadha = also Bhabru.
  • RE IV = Dhammaghosha replaces Bherighosa. RE XIII = Kalinga war edict.
  • Barabar Hills caves = for Ajivikas. Nagarjuni Hills = Dasaratha built.
  • Ptolemy of Egypt sent Dionysius as ambassador to Ashoka's court.
  • According to Puranas, Mauryan dynasty lasted 137 years.
  • Brihadratha was the last Mauryan ruler (ruled 7 years).
Ashoka's two Bhabru facts: 1) Reverence for Buddhism 2) Claims to be emperor of Magadha. ONE edict, TWO exam answers!

🔄 Post-Maurya Dynasties

Pushyamitra CONTINUED to be called SENANI (commander) — not king! He was the first Brahmanical ruler. Kanvas were ended by ANDHRAS (Satavahanas), not Guptas.

Shunga Dynasty 🔥

  • Founded by Pushyamitra Shunga — Mauryan commander-in-chief. Killed Brihadratha (last Mauryan, ruled 7 years).
  • Capital: Pataliputra. First Brahmanical state in Magadha.
  • Patanjali (wrote Mahabhashya) was contemporary of Pushyamitra.
  • Gargi Samhita = Greek (Yavana) attack on Pataliputra during Pushyamitra's reign.
  • Bharhut and Sanchi monuments erected/embellished during Shunga rule.
  • Heliodorus (ambassador of Antialkidas) erected Garuda pillar at Besnagar during Bhadraka's rule.
  • Agnimitra (son) = viceroy at Vidisha. Vidarbha war = important event.
Shunga = First Brahmanical rulers. Patanjali + Pushyamitra = contemporaries. Bharhut + Sanchi = Shunga monuments!

Later Guptas + Aryabhata

  • Jivitagupta II — last Later Gupta ruler.
  • Madhavagupta (Later Gupta) — representative of Harshavardhana in Magadha.
