🧠 Mnemonics

🧠 Memory Tricks — Bihar Geography

Rivers (North Bank tributaries of Ganga): "Ganda Bag Mahananda Kosi"
→ Gandak + Bagmati + Mahananda + Kosi — all north-bank rivers originating from Nepal/Himalayas
Kosi = "Kosi Often Shifts Inward" (KOSI)
→ The Kosi river has shifted its course westward by ~120 km in 250 years, hence "Sorrow of Bihar"
9 Divisions: "Patna Tigers Sip Drinks, Keep Pouring Bourbon Morning Milk"
→ Patna · Tirhut · Saran · Darbhanga · Koshi · Purnia · Bhagalpur · Munger · Magadh
South-bank rivers: "Son Punpun Falgu" → SPF (Sun Protection Factor!) — they flow from the south plateau
→ Son (MP), Punpun (Jharkhand), Falgu (Jharkhand) — all drain the Chhotanagpur/Vindhya regions
📌 PYQ Spotlight

📌 BPSC PYQ Spotlight — Geography

Q1. Which river is known as the "Sorrow of Bihar"?
✅ Answer: Kosi River
Kosi is notorious for its highly mobile channel — it has shifted ~120 km westward in 250 years, causing devastating floods.
BPSC 54th, 63rd variants
Q2. The only National Park in Bihar is located in which district?
✅ Answer: West Champaran (Valmiki National Park)
Valmiki NP is part of the Valmiki Tiger Reserve. It borders Nepal and covers ~900 km². It is Bihar's only National Park.
BPSC 65th, 68th
Q3. At which place do the Gandak and Ganga rivers meet?
✅ Answer: Hajipur / Sonepur (Vaishali/Saran district)
The Gandak–Ganga confluence at Sonepur is famous for the Sonepur Cattle Fair (Haririval Mela), Asia's largest cattle fair.
BPSC GK standard
Q4. How many districts does Bihar have as of 2024?
✅ Answer: 38 districts
Bihar has 38 districts organised into 9 divisions. The newest district is Arwal (carved from Jehanabad in 2001).
BPSC standard
Q5. Vikramshila Gangetic Dolphin Sanctuary is located in which district?
✅ Answer: Bhagalpur
It spans a 60-km stretch of the Ganga between Sultanganj and Kahalgaon in Bhagalpur district. It protects the endangered Gangetic River Dolphin (Bihar's state animal).
BPSC 64th, 69th

📌 PYQ Spotlight

📌 BPSC PYQ Spotlight — Economy

Q1. Which product of Bihar received GI tag and is related to Mithila region?
✅ Answer: Mithila Makhana (Fox Nut / Water Chestnut)
Mithila Makhana received GI tag in 2022. India produces ~90% of world's Makhana, mostly from Bihar's Mithila region (Darbhanga, Madhubani, Sitamarhi).
BPSC 70th pattern
Q2. Bhagalpur is famous for which type of silk?
✅ Answer: Tussar Silk (also called Tasar or Kosa Silk)
Bhagalpur is called "Silk City" of Bihar. Tussar is a wild silk produced from Antheraea mylitta silkworm. It is coarser than Mulberry silk but highly valued for its natural golden sheen.
BPSC 65th, 69th
Q3. Silao is located in which district and what is it famous for?
✅ Answer: Nalanda district; famous for Silao Khaja (a sweet)
Silao is on the Patna–Rajgir highway. Khaja is a multi-layered deep-fried sweet with GI tag, historically popular with pilgrims visiting Nalanda/Rajgir.
BPSC standard GK
Q4. Bihar ranks second in India in the production of which crop?
✅ Answer: Maize (corn)
Bihar is India's 2nd largest maize producer after Karnataka. The Kosi region (Supaul, Saharsa, Madhepura) is the maize belt. It is also a major centre for maize-based ethanol production.
BPSC 68th, 70th

📌 PYQ Spotlight

📌 BPSC PYQ Spotlight — Census

Q1. As per Census 2011, what is the population density of Bihar?
✅ Answer: 1,106 persons per sq km
Bihar's density (1106/km²) is almost 3x the national average (382/km²), making it one of the most densely populated states in India.
BPSC 64th, 67th, 69th
Q2. Which district of Bihar has the highest sex ratio as per Census 2011?
✅ Answer: Gopalganj (1,021 females per 1000 males)
Gopalganj is notable for having a sex ratio higher than 1000 (i.e., more women than men), likely due to high male out-migration for work.
BPSC 69th, 70th pattern
Q3. What is the literacy rate of Bihar as per Census 2011?
✅ Answer: 61.8%
Bihar's overall literacy (61.8%) is below national average (74%). Female literacy is particularly low at 51.5%. Rohtas district leads with ~73.4% literacy.
BPSC standard; 68th, 70th
Q4. Which is the least urbanised state in India as per Census 2011?
✅ Answer: Bihar (urban population only ~11.3%)
Bihar has the lowest urban population share in India. This is important for questions on rural-urban divide, migration, and urbanisation trends.
BPSC 65th, 70th

📌 PYQ Spotlight — History

Q1. Where did Mahatma Gandhi conduct his first Satyagraha in India?
✅ Answer: Champaran, Bihar (1917)
The Champaran Satyagraha (1917) was against the Tinkathia system forcing farmers to grow indigo on 3/20 of their land. Gandhi arrived on invitation of Raj Kumar Shukla.
BPSC 54th, 63rd, 68th, 70th
Q2. Nalanda University was destroyed by whom?
✅ Answer: Bakhtiyar Khilji (c. 1200 CE)
Turkish commander Bakhtiyar Khilji destroyed Nalanda and its famous library in ~1200 CE. The university was a major centre of Buddhist learning for ~700 years.
BPSC standard
Q3. Jharkhand was carved out of Bihar on which date?
✅ Answer: 15 November 2000
November 15, 2000 is also the birth anniversary of Birsa Munda. Jharkhand (29th state) was formed along with Chhattisgarh and Uttarakhand in 2000.
BPSC 64th, 69th
Q4. Which language spoken in Bihar is included in the Eighth Schedule of the Constitution?
✅ Answer: Maithili (added in 2003 by 92nd Amendment)
Maithili was added to the 8th Schedule in 2003 as the 22nd language. It is mainly spoken in the Mithila region (N Bihar). Bhojpuri and Magahi are NOT in the 8th Schedule.
BPSC 65th, 70th

📌 PYQ Spotlight — Polity

Q1. How many seats does Bihar have in the Lok Sabha?
✅ Answer: 40 seats
Bihar has 40 Lok Sabha seats, making it the 4th largest state by LS representation (after UP-80, Maharashtra-48, WB-42). This is a frequent trap question.
BPSC 69th, 70th
Q2. How many seats are there in Bihar Legislative Council (Vidhan Parishad)?
✅ Answer: 75 seats
The Bihar Vidhan Parishad (upper house) has 75 members. It is a permanent body — one-third retire every 2 years. Members are elected/nominated by various constituencies including MLAs, teachers, graduates, and Governor's nominations.
BPSC 65th, 68th
Q3. Under which Article of the Constitution is the Bihar Public Service Commission established?
✅ Answer: Article 315
Article 315 mandates establishment of a Public Service Commission for each State. BPSC was established in 1951 and is headquartered in Patna.
BPSC standard

📌 PYQ Spotlight — Schemes

Q1. Under Bihar Student Credit Card scheme, what is the maximum loan amount?
✅ Answer: ₹4 lakh at 4% interest (1% for women/transgender/differently-abled)
The Bihar Student Credit Card scheme provides educational loans up to ₹4 lakh with very low interest (4%) for students who passed Class 12 to pursue higher education.
BPSC 69th, 70th
Q2. What is the percentage of reservation for women in Bihar government jobs under 7 Nischay?
✅ Answer: 35%
Bihar provides 35% reservation for women in government jobs under the 6th pillar of 7 Nischay Part 1 (Arakshit Rozgar Mahilaon Ka Adhikar). This is one of the highest in India.
BPSC standard
