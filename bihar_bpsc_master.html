🧠 Mnemonics

🧠 Memory Tricks — Bihar Geography

North-bank rivers: "Ganda Bag Mahananda Kosi"
→ Gandak + Bagmati + Mahananda + Kosi — all north-bank rivers originating from Nepal/Himalayas
Kosi = "Kosi Often Shifts Inward" (KOSI)
→ Shifted ~120 km westward in 250 years — hence "Sorrow of Bihar"
9 Divisions: "Patna Tigers Sip Drinks, Keep Pouring Bourbon Morning Milk"
→ Patna · Tirhut · Saran · Darbhanga · Koshi · Purnia · Bhagalpur · Munger · Magadh
South-bank rivers: "Son Punpun Falgu" → SPF (Sun Protection Factor!)
→ Son (MP), Punpun (Jharkhand), Falgu (Jharkhand) — all drain from south plateau
📌 PYQ Spotlight

📌 BPSC PYQ Spotlight — Geography

Q1. Which river is known as the "Sorrow of Bihar"?
✅ Kosi River
Kosi's highly mobile channel — shifted ~120 km westward in 250 years, causing devastating floods annually.
BPSC 54th, 63rd
Q2. The only National Park in Bihar is located in which district?
✅ West Champaran — Valmiki National Park
Valmiki NP is part of Valmiki Tiger Reserve. Borders Nepal, covers ~900 km². Bihar's only National Park.
BPSC 65th, 68th
Q3. At which place do Gandak and Ganga rivers meet?
✅ Hajipur / Sonepur (Vaishali/Saran)
The Gandak–Ganga confluence at Sonepur hosts Asia's largest cattle fair (Haririval Mela) at Kartik Purnima.
BPSC standard
Q4. How many districts does Bihar have?
✅ 38 districts
38 districts in 9 divisions. Newest: Arwal (carved from Jehanabad, 2001).
BPSC standard
Q5. Vikramshila Gangetic Dolphin Sanctuary — district?
✅ Bhagalpur
60-km stretch of Ganga (Sultanganj to Kahalgaon). Protects Bihar's state animal — Gangetic River Dolphin.
BPSC 64th, 69th

📌 PYQ Spotlight

📌 BPSC PYQ Spotlight — Economy

Q1. Which Bihar GI product is related to the Mithila region, GI tag 2022?
✅ Mithila Makhana (Fox Nut)
India produces ~90% of world's Makhana. Mithila region: Darbhanga, Madhubani, Sitamarhi, Supaul.
BPSC 70th
Q2. Bhagalpur is famous for which type of silk?
✅ Tussar Silk (Tasar/Kosa Silk)
Wild silk from Antheraea mylitta silkworm; coarser than Mulberry but valued for natural golden sheen.
BPSC 65th, 69th
Q3. Bihar ranks second in India in production of which crop?
✅ Maize (corn)
2nd after Karnataka. Kosi region (Supaul, Saharsa, Madhepura) is the maize belt. Major ethanol source.
BPSC 68th, 70th
Q4. Silao Khaja — which district?
✅ Nalanda district
Silao town on Patna–Rajgir highway. Multi-layered flaky GI-tagged sweet, popular with pilgrims.
BPSC standard

Bihar History & Culture

⚠️ History Traps
Champaran = first Satyagraha in India (South Africa 1906 was the first ever). Bhojpuri NOT in 8th Schedule — Maithili is. Jharkhand = 15 Nov 2000 (Chhattisgarh = 1 Nov).

📌 PYQ Spotlight

📌 PYQ Spotlight — History

Q1. Gandhi's first Satyagraha in India?
✅ Champaran, Bihar (1917)
Against Tinkathia indigo system. Gandhi invited by Raj Kumar Shukla.
BPSC 54th, 63rd, 68th, 70th
Q2. Nalanda University destroyed by?
✅ Bakhtiyar Khilji (~1200 CE)
Turkic commander burned the famous library. Ended ~700 years of Buddhist scholarship.
BPSC standard
Q3. Jharkhand carved from Bihar on?
✅ 15 November 2000 (Birsa Munda's birth anniversary)
29th state. Chhattisgarh: 1 Nov. Uttarakhand: 9 Nov. All three formed in 2000.
BPSC 64th, 69th
Q4. Which Bihar language is in 8th Schedule?
✅ Maithili (92nd Amendment, 2003 — 22nd language)
Bhojpuri and Magahi are NOT in 8th Schedule. Common BPSC trap.
BPSC 65th, 70th

📌 PYQ Spotlight

📌 PYQ Spotlight — Polity

Q1. Bihar's Lok Sabha seats?
✅ 40 seats (4th highest nationally)
UP(80) > Maha(48) > WB(42) > Bihar(40). WB is ahead of Bihar — frequent trap.
BPSC 69th, 70th
Q2. Bihar Vidhan Parishad seats?
✅ 75 seats — permanent body, NOT dissolved with Vidhan Sabha
Like Rajya Sabha — permanent. 1/3 members retire every 2 years. 6-year term.
BPSC 65th, 68th
Q3. BPSC established under which Article?
✅ Article 315 — established 1951
Art 315 mandates state PSC. Art 316 = appointment of chairman by Governor.
BPSC standard
